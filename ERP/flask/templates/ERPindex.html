What is ERP?

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is a type of software that helps organizations manage and integrate their important business processes.
It allows them to streamline and automate tasks related to finance, human resources, supply chain, manufacturing, and more, using a centralized database and a set of interconnected applications.


Any Questions



Enterprise: Refers to a large organization or company, typically one that operates on a national or international scale.

Resource: Refers to any asset, capability, process, information, or knowledge that is owned or controlled by an organization and can be used to achieve its objectives.

Planning: Refers to the process of setting goals, defining strategies, and outlining tasks to achieve those goals efficiently.
